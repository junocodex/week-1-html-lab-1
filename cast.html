Cast

  • Ray Liotta as Henry Hill
  • Robert De Niro as James "Jimmy" Conway
  • Joe Pesci as Tommy DeVito
  • Lorraine Bracco as Karen Hill
  • Paul Sorvino as Paulie Cicero
  • Samuel L. Jackson as Stacks Edwards
  • Raymond J. Barry as Morrie Kessler
  • Ray Liotta – Known for his breakthrough role in Goodfellas, Liotta gained fame for his portrayal of Henry Hill. He also starred in Field of Dreams and appeared in various TV shows and films throughout his career.
  • Robert De Niro – An iconic American actor known for his collaborations with Martin Scorsese. He is celebrated for roles in Taxi Driver, The Godfather Part II, and Raging Bull, often portraying intense and complex characters.
  • Joe Pesci – A versatile actor famous for his roles in Goodfellas, Home Alone, and My Cousin Vinny. Known for playing both comedic and dangerous characters with equal skill, he earned an Oscar for his role in Goodfellas.
  • Lorraine Bracco – An accomplished actress best known for playing Dr. Jennifer Melfi in The Sopranos. She also starred in Goodfellas, gaining recognition for her role as Henry Hill's wife, Karen.
  • Paul Sorvino – A veteran actor with a career spanning decades, Sorvino is known for his role as Paulie Cicero in Goodfellas and his performances in Reds and The Firm. He is also a respected voice in opera.
  • Samuel L. Jackson – One of the most prolific actors of his generation, Jackson is famous for his roles in Pulp Fiction, The Avengers franchise, and Django Unchained. His commanding presence and diverse roles have made him a Hollywood legend.
  • Raymond J. Barry – An American actor known for his versatile roles in both film and television. He is best recognized for his portrayal of the villainous "Uncle Junior" in The Sandlot (1993) and for his roles in The West Wing and Justified. Barry has appeared in numerous dramas and thrillers, often playing intense and complex characters.
